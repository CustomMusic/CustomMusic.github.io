The Process

A great song can transform any video from background noise to an emotional, motivating message. Unfortunately, shrinking budgets limit your search for the perfect track, as traditional music houses can be expensive and cheap production music sounds generic. CrucialCustom allows you to control the process and license custom music affordably. Here's how it works…

  1. You pick the licensing terms, provide project and music details, and upload your video.
  2. We match your project with talented custom music songwriters who upload original demos synched to picture.
  3. We curate and deliver only those demos that meet our highest quality standards.
  4. You pick 5 tracks you love, and work directly with each artist to get them just right.
  5. You pick a winner, pay by credit card or PO and download the music and executed license.

Curated Talent

CrucialCustom brings the best-of-the-best indie musicians together in an invite-only community…meaning anyone who touches your project is an expert in the desired genre…and can deliver vocal songs just as easy as instrumentals. We curate the entire demo process to make sure what you hear is the best you’re going to hear anywhere.

Seasoned Veterans

Mentor Jim Long and mentee Tanvi Patel, supported by a staff of music aficionados, have run indie artist catalog CrucialMusic for almost a decade. Their experience includes placing a multitude of music in blockbuster films, Emmy winning shows and big brand spots. Now they’re leveraging the brand and its premium talent pool to deliver quality custom music at a reasonable price to the internet ad market.
