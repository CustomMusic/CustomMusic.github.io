CrucialCustom Launches Online Custom Music Marketplace for Ads

January 28, 2016

Posted in: Music, Television, Advertising, Press Releases

CrucialCustom connects music supervisors, agencies and brands to a vetted network of independent composers and musicians via a custom music licensing platform where they can solicit custom tracks for TV commercials, online and mobile video and industrial uses including corporate presentations and in-store videos.
